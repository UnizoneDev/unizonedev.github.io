Unizone Realms

A website created by me.


I create YTPs and YTMND-esque image and video edits, mod old games such as Doom and Serious Sam, and make my own music, textures, models, etc.


Projects I've Worked On

Massacre 3D, the GZDoom mod that mocks the internet and its various fandoms, along with religion, politics, and stereotypes.

Abyssal Fear, the Serious Sam 1 engine game based on Dante's Inferno.

Destructo-Beats, a sample library that's a tribute to Big Fish Audio's Titanium Rhythms 1 and 2, which were created by Jason Jones.
